Your Shopping Companion

From Discovery to Purchase

Find a product you love, and let our agent take over. We'll help you find the best time to buy, so you can make a confident decision.

Real-Time Guidance

Set a Target, We'll Handle the Rest.

No more manual price checking. Our agent monitors prices in real-time. We surface preset, data-driven targets to give you a trustworthy baseline, while still allowing for full customization. In our testing, users overwhelmingly preferred this guided approach.

User Control

You're Always in the Driver's Seat.

Automation is scary if it feels out of control. That's why we designed a critical choice into the core experience: "Buy Automatically" or "Notify Me to Confirm". This simple opt-in ensures you have the final say, building confidence and trust in the agent.

Smart Adjustments

Adapts to Market Changes

If the likelihood of a price drop gets low, the system responds. Our agent will recommend adjustments to your target to improve your chances of success, keeping you informed every step of the way.

Seamless Experience

From Purchase to Placement

Once the purchase is made, you get instant confirmation and order details. The agent keeps you in the loop, so you always know the status of your order without having to check multiple sites.

Full Journey Support

Post-Purchase Peace of Mind

After the purchase is completed, the agent has your warranty details and other information for future use. This allows for post-purchase help, such as recall alerts or repair guides, turning a one-time transaction into a long-term relationship.

How It Works: An Agentic AI Experience

This experience is powered by agentic AI—an agent that does tasks on your behalf. While the technology exists today, it's often used to increase profit, not to serve the consumer. We leverage CR's brand of trust to build an agent that is truly on your side.

Robust Price Data

Our agent needs vast amounts of data. We can partner with best-in-class services like Price API and CamelCamelCamel for rapid market entry, or build this capability internally over time.

Web Automation

To purchase on a consumer's behalf, we can use powerful web automation tools like Selenium or Playwright to navigate retailer sites and complete transactions securely and reliably.

Dynamic Probability Engine

Our probability score is created dynamically using historical price trends and current market data. If the chance of a price drop falls below a certain threshold (e.g., 30%), the agent proactively recommends alternative price and time targets.
